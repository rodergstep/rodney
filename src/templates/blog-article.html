We’re hiring! Take your hairdressing career to the next level

02 September 2018
Share to Facebook
Do you love helping people look and feel fantastic? We invite you to further your hairdressing career as part of the team at New Zealand’s most awarded hair salon brand, Rodney Wayne.

At Rodney Wayne, we believe everyone has the right to look and feel fabulous. We care for our clients’ hair without compromising the health or integrity of their hair. To deliver on this promise takes people who are passionate about hairdressing and keen to bring out the best in themselves and others. Does this sound like you? Then we’d like to welcome you to the team.
You’ll love working at Rodney Wayne
We love working here and we know that you will too. Here’s why:

Great working environment

Rodney Wayne salons are dynamic, modern and well equipped. We use and recommend Kérastase, Redken, ghd and L’Oreal Professionnel products. Our people love hairdressing and are motivated, positive and supportive. We have a great mix of customers throughout New Zealand who rely on us for beautiful healthy hair.

World class hairdressing

Learn from some of the best! Rodney Wayne creative director Richard Kavanagh is renowned as one of the world’s top session stylists. He is a global ambassador for Redken and styles for fashion weeks and hair and fashion magazines around the world. Richard leads many of our training programmes and ensures our award-winning hair stylists and colourists are at the forefront of hairdressing. You too could join the many Rodney Wayne hair stylists and colourists who are garnering local and international acclaim.

Excellent training and support

You’ll benefit from our industry-leading hairdressing training programs and support systems while earning an excellent income. The Rodney Wayne Rewards programme gives your clients extra rewards, special offers and surprises so they feel more positive, and visit more often.

Flexible schedules if needed

Everyone has different working requirements. So we have full-time and part-time positions available at our salons across New Zealand.

Earn more at Rodney Wayne

We know you love hairdressing but we also know that talent deserves to be rewarded. At Rodney Wayne, we offer performance incentives and generous team awards on top of pay and bonuses.

Career growth

Watch your career flourish at Rodney Wayne! We have a range of development programs and pathways to develop your potential. There are multiple opportunities for career growth, from hair technician to stylist, to store manager and salon owner.

Apply now!
If you love to pamper your clients, enjoy working within a team environment and are motivated to learn about creating great hair then contact us now. There is a range of exciting hairdressing careers and hair salon team and leadership roles for you at Rodney Wayne and Rodney Wayne Shampoo ‘n’ Things. There are Rodney Wayne salons and Shampoo ‘n’ Things retail stores conveniently located throughout New Zealand. We are always looking for new Rodney Wayne team members.

We look forward to hearing from you.
