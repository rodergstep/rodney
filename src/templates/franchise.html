Franchise Opportunities

Rodney Wayne invites you to join this iconic New Zealand brand. If you are a motivated ‘people person’ and curious about the hair industry, this is the opportunity for you.

Become a Franchise Owner

Rodney Wayne is looking for new franchise owners across New Zealand to be a part of the plan for growth. It’s an exciting and rewarding industry, and becoming a Rodney Wayne salon and Shampoo ’n’ Things owner comes with a range of unique additional benefits.

  • Mission and values that drive enriched customer experience and business growth.
  • Trusted and progressive brand.
  • Proven system for success.
  • Industry-leading training and support, including financial management benchmarking and staff education to help keep your business at peak performance.
  • World-class marketing and customer engagement programme. Superior products.
  • Strong buying power.

Once you join Rodney Wayne, you’ll also have a dedicated support manager to guide you through all aspects of the business.

Join the team

Franchise opportunities are available in Auckland, Wellington, Christchurch, Tauranga, Queenstown and Dunedin from $150k to over $500k. A hairdressing background is not essential.
