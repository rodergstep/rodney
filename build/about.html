Rodney Wayne is recognised as the most awarded hair salon in New Zealand. Winner of Hairstylist of the Year at the Fashion Hair Awards 2018.

Rodney Wayne is dedicated to providing you with world-leading hairstyling, hair colouring and hair care products.

Stylists from our salons around NZ also dominated this prestigious show. Visit us now for an individual consultation with an expert hairdresser. Authorised L’Oréal Colour Ambassadors are available in every one of our salons. Our stylists and colourists are trained to enhance the beauty of your hair without compromising the health or integrity of your hair. And you’ll be pampered with the highest quality hair care products including genuine L’Oreal Professionnel colours and Kérastase treatments.

Rodney Wayne opened his first Auckland salon in the heart of the city in 1980. , hair colouring and hair care products.

The luxurious salon immediately captured the attention of Aucklanders seeking world-class style. Within eight years, Rodney was operating eight hair salons. He knew that a quality consultation was vital to understanding and achieving client needs and wishes. ‘We listen. We care’ became a guiding principle. Great service and even better hair were hallmarks.

Now there are over 30 salons in the Rodney Wayne stable and 15 Shampoo ‘n’ Things haircare stores across the country.

Richard Kavanagh

As the global creative director for Rodney Wayne, Richard develops and interpret global trends for our stylists through seasonal campaign shoots. “I’m privileged to be able to create an education and coaching program for the elite group of artists at Rodney Wayne. This talented crew help make the wonderful women we see each week, feel good about themselves. It is my passion to develop and fuel an award-winning culture within the group. My inspiration comes from travelling the globe, creating hairstyles for fashion advertising, editorials and fashion week shows.” enthuses Richard.

Richard’s clients include the likes of Vogue, Harpers Bazaar, Redken, Zambesi, Kate Sylvester, Kelly Osbourne, Adrian Brody and Lorde.

The Rodney Wayne Artistic Team are experts in their craft.

They receive high-level training and are selected through a rigorous audition process.
