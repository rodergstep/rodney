Page Boy

by Andrew Cobeldick, Rodney Wayne artistic team

70s styles are back and the ‘Page Boy’ was so beautiful in the era and due for a comeback. We wanted to take it to the next level, make it new and fresh. The Colour is bright and beautiful with deep root colour. The yellow/gold colour with violet hues was created to be elegant, wearable every day while pushing the boundaries. Clashing two opposite colours that end up complimenting and working in harmony.”

Get this look

Andrew’s Golden Page Boy cut is created with INOA colour and protected with Smartbond by L’Oréal Professionnel. Styled with ghd Curve. See your Rodney Wayne stylist for more information.

Copper Locks

by Lizzie White, Rodney Wayne artistic team

When I met Tessa I immediately saw that courage in her face, and I wanted to take her curls from fluff to strong-bodied and polished and give her copper locks a boost. Enhancing your natural look is often the most flattering for your personal colour palette when it comes to skin tones and eye colour - drawing out the best you.

Get this look

Add a few drops of Redken Diamond Oil, and a nourishing mousse like Redken Full Effect. To enhance the copper shade with a glossy tone, use the L’Oreal INOA ammonia free colouring range. For something less permanent try Kérastase Touche Chromatique Copper.

Smooth & Seamless

by Newton Cook, Rodney Wayne artistic team

I’m inspired by healthy hair. The concept of healthy hair is usually established with length and shine, so I like to achieve the smoothest and sleekest hair possible. To create a look that a lot of women are eager to have means a lot to me.

Get this look
